About me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio.
I’m Thibaud Bergez-Vigneau, I’m 19, and on this page you will find information about multiple programming related projects of mine, some finished, some in progress.

I also like to write code related to images and music, and I plan to make a program that can create generative music, as well as a music visualizer.

In the future I plan to continue making side projects related to images and music, in languages I don’t know yet, like C++ or Rust, if they are suited for the projects.

Studies
