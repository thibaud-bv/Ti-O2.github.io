Personnal Life

Hello and welcome to my portfolio. I’m Thibaud Bergez-Vigneau, I’m 19, and on this page you will find information about the different programming related projects I have done, some finished, some in progress. In my free time I like to listen to music, especially stuff like experimental electronic music. I like to go to the flea-market early in the morning to hopefully find interesting old electronic equipement or cassettes to listen to. I also like to program stuff related to images and music, and I plan to make a program that can create generative music, as well as a music visualizer. I speak French, English and Spanish, which allows me to communicate rather easily with people from other countries. I also plan on learning German, if I have the time. In the future I plan to continue making side projects related to arts, in languages I don’t know yet, like C++ or Rust, if they are suited for the projects.

Studies

I am currently a second year student at the IUT de Bordeaux in France, in the Computer Science department. Before that I went to Saint-Genès La Salle High School in Bordeaux, where I took Mathematics and Computer Science (NSI, Numérique et Sciences de l’Informatique) as principal subjects. During the last year I took the “Advanced Mathematics” option to strengthen my mathematical knowledge and feed my curiosity. I was part of the “European Class”, which had added English classes. Following this, I chose to take part in the International Track option of the BUT Informatique, with the goal of doing one or multiple internships abroad.
